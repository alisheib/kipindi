Step 1 · Sign up

Create your account

You need a Tanzanian phone number, a password, and to be 18 or older. The whole sign-up takes under a minute.

Sign-up screen · Open 50pick.tz and tap "Create account".

1

Enter your phone number

Type only digits — the input strips letters or spaces automatically and shows the number formatted as 712 345 678. The "+255" prefix is added for you.

Andika tarakimu tu — alama nyingine zinaondolewa moja kwa moja.

2

Pick a strong password

At least 8 characters. The strength meter under the field tells you when it's Strong · Imara. Avoid common passwords like "password" or "12345678" — the platform refuses them.

3

Confirm age & terms

Tick the two boxes — you must be 18 or older and you accept the Terms and Privacy notice. The optional third box opts you in to product updates.

Step 2 · Browse markets

Find a question to predict

Markets are real-world questions with a YES or NO answer — football, weather, macro, crypto, culture. The bar on each card shows where the pool is leaning right now.

Markets · Tap any card to see the full question and place a bet.

1

Filter by category

Use the chips at the top — All, Sports, Macro, Weather, Crypto, Culture — to narrow down.

2

Read the bar

Green is YES, red is NO. The percentages tell you where the crowd has put their money. A market near 50/50 is "tipping" — the most contested.

3

Tap to open

Tap any card to see the full question, the resolution source, the live pool, and the bet panel.

Step 3 · Place a bet

The conviction dial

Market detail · Drag the dial toward YES (right) or NO (left). The projected payout updates live.

1

Drag the dial

Slide right for YES, left for NO. The further you push, the bigger your stake. A small dot in the centre means "no bet yet."

2

Read the projection

The screen shows your stake, the projected payout if you're right, and the operator margin (the same nine percent on every market).

3

Confirm

Tap Place bet. A confirmation pops up with a five-second locked-in price. Tap Confirm to submit. You'll get an inbox notification right away with the receipt.

Quote-hold. The five-second timer means the price you see at confirmation is the price you get — no slippage, no surprise. If you wait too long, the modal closes and you can re-aim.

Step 4 · Wallet & positions

Track your money

Your wallet shows your balance, deposit and withdrawal history. The top-bar pill (TZS pill next to your avatar) is the same number — tap it any time.

Wallet · Deposit, withdraw, history, and the AML notice when limits apply.

Open bets live on the Positions screen. Each card shows the market, your side (YES or NO), the stake, and the projected payout if you win.

Positions · Open bets and resolved bets, in one list.

Step 5 · Profile & safety

You're in control

Your Profile screen carries everything that's about you — display name, KYC status, language, region, the limits you've set on yourself.

Profile · Your account, your limits, your sessions.

If gambling stops being fun, stop.

The Responsible gambling screen lets you set deposit limits (daily, weekly, monthly), session limits, take a break (1 hour, 24 hours, 1 week), or self-exclude (24 hours through permanent). Self-exclusion cannot be reversed by you until the period ends — that's the whole point.

Responsible gambling · Set limits before you need them.
