Step 1 · Sign up

Create your account

You need a Tanzanian phone number, a password, and to be 18 or older. Under a minute end-to-end.

Sign-up · Open 50pick.tz and tap "Create account".

1

Phone number

Digits only — letters and spaces are stripped automatically. The "+255" prefix is added for you. The field shows the number formatted as 712 345 678.

Andika tarakimu tu — alama nyingine zinaondolewa moja kwa moja.

2

Strong password

At least 8 characters. The strength meter shows Strong · Imara when you're there. Common passwords are refused.

3

Age & terms

Tick the two required boxes. The third (product updates) is optional.

After Create account you land on the verify-identity screen. A welcome message confirms your account is open.

Verify identity · Unlocks withdrawals.

Stay safe. Never share your password — not with support, friends, or family. If you forget it, use the Forgot password link on the sign-in page.

Step 2 · Browse markets & place a bet

Predict, stake, share the pool

Markets are real-world questions with a YES or NO answer — football, weather, macro, crypto, culture. The bar on each card shows where the pool is leaning right now.

Markets · Filter by category, read the bar, tap to open.

1

Filter

Use the chips at the top — All, Sports, Macro, Weather, Crypto, Culture.

2

Read the bar

Green is YES, red is NO. A market near 50/50 is "tipping" — the most contested.

The conviction dial

Drag the dial toward YES or NO. The projection updates live.

3

Drag

Right for YES, left for NO. The further you push, the bigger your stake.

4

Confirm

Tap Place bet. A 5-second locked-in price pops up — tap Confirm. You'll get an inbox notification with the receipt.

Quote-hold. The 5-second timer means the price you see at confirmation is the price you get — no slippage. Wait too long and the modal closes so you can re-aim.

Step 3 · Wallet & positions

Track your money

Your wallet shows your balance, deposits, and withdrawals. The TZS pill in the top bar (next to your avatar) is the same number — tap it any time.

Wallet · Deposit, withdraw, history, AML notice.

Open bets live on Positions. Each card shows the market, your side (YES or NO), the stake, and the projected payout if you win.

Positions · Open and resolved bets, one list.

Step 4 · Profile & safety

You're in control

Your Profile carries everything about you — display name, KYC status, language, region, the limits you've set on yourself.

Profile · Account, limits, sessions.

If gambling stops being fun, stop.

The Responsible gambling screen lets you set deposit limits (daily / weekly / monthly), session limits, take a break (1 h / 24 h / 1 wk), or self-exclude (24 h through permanent). Self-exclusion cannot be reversed by you until the period ends — that's the whole point.

Responsible gambling · Set limits before you need them.
