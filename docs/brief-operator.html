Executive summary

What 50pick is

50pick is a pari-mutuel prediction market built for Tanzania. Players ask "yes or no" on a real-world question — "Will Simba SC win the Kariakoo Derby?" or "Will the long rains start before April 15?" — stake what they believe, and share the pool with everyone who got it right. Losers' stakes flow to winners minus a transparent operator margin. No house edge, no bookmaker setting the price.

Why this matters

Tanzania has more than sixty million people, mobile-money penetration above seventy percent, and a legal sports-betting market growing in the high single digits each year. Existing operators run a fixed-odds bookmaker stance — the house sets the price; the house wins on average. 50pick flips the model: the players themselves set the price by where they stake. The operator earns a clean nine percent of resolved volume, audited and visible. That alignment is what makes a regulator comfortable and a player feel respected.

Production posture

Built end-to-end against the Tanzania Gaming Board pre-application checklist and aligned with international gambling-industry standards (LCCP, ISO 27001, OWASP). Phone-OTP sign-in, NIDA-backed identity verification, mobile-money deposit and withdrawal, real-time market pools, two-officer settlement, append-only audit trail, full responsible-gambling controls. Mobile-first across every screen, bilingual from day one.

Two-officer settlement

Market resolution requires two independent officer sign-offs. The same officer can never confirm twice. Stage one stages the outcome; stage two settles.

AML queue

Transactions over the FATF threshold queue for a compliance officer's review. Approval or escalation is logged with reason.

Tamper-evident audit

Every state change, login attempt, wallet movement, and admin decision is hash-chained. Any modified entry breaks the chain on next verify.

Competitive position

Why 50pick wins

Tanzania's regulated betting market is dominated by operators running the same fixed-odds football product. Switching costs are near zero — the player picks whichever app loads fastest. 50pick's differentiation isn't a marketing claim; it's structural.

Three structural advantages

Pari-mutuel

The house never bets against the player. Players' stakes pool together; the operator earns a transparent fixed margin off the resolved volume. The model is the same one Hong Kong Jockey Club has used since 1971 with no modern bankruptcies in the model. International prediction markets ran billions in annualised volume on a comparable framework.

Wider scope

Football still drives the bulk of acquired traffic. But weather (long rains begin before April 15? — every farmer cares), macro (TZS-USD month-end?), crypto, and culture markets attract a category of player our incumbents can't reach. Each category re-engages the same wallet.

Compliance-led

The product was built backwards from the Tanzania Gaming Board's pre-application checklist. Every screen, every back-end action, every audit entry was designed to pass a regulator's first inspection without re-architecting. Incumbents are retrofitting compliance; we shipped with it.
