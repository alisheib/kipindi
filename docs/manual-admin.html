Section 1

Daily operations

Overview

Overview · live snapshot

The Overview tile in the sidebar opens the operations dashboard — live volume, predictor counts, market health, recent activity at one glance.

Every other area is one click away in the sidebar; every action you take is logged to the audit trail with your user ID.

Finance · daily reconciliation

Open the Finance tile from the sidebar. Stakes, payouts, operator margin, withholding tax — filterable by day or month.

  • Reconcile platform payouts against the aggregator each morning.
  • Track operator margin (9% of resolved volume) week-over-week.
  • CSV export per period for board reports and regulator returns.

Finance

Players

Players · search & drill in

Open the Players tile from the sidebar. Search by phone, ID, or display name. Drill in to see wallet, KYC, positions, RG limits, and the audit trail tied to that user.

PII discipline. Never copy a player's phone or NIDA into chat or email. Every drill-in is logged.

Section 2

Markets & risk

Markets

Markets · create & manage

Open the Markets tile from the sidebar. Tap + New market to launch one. Question (EN + SW), category, source URL (must be on the approved registry), and resolution time.

Edit, void, or archive any existing market from the same list.

Resolver · two-officer rule

Open the Resolver queue from the sidebar. Markets within 24 hours of their resolution time appear here. Stage 1 stages the outcome; Stage 2 settles. The same officer cannot confirm twice.

Settlement is final. Once Stage 2 fires, payouts credit and positions close. No undo.

For a live walk-through, use the Demo · 5-minute or 15-minute markets at the top of the player Markets page (sorted by Ending soon) — place a bet, then return here to settle and watch the win celebration on the player browser.

Resolver

AML

AML · threshold review

Open the AML queue from the sidebar. Transactions above the FATF reporting threshold are paused here.

Open each one, review history, then approve (logged with reason) or escalate to the FIU. Nothing settles without a recorded sign-off.

Section 3

Compliance & integrity

Self-exclusions

Self-exclusions · monitor only

Open the Self-exclusions tile from the sidebar. Active exclusions, end dates, reasons.

The platform refuses login and bet attempts automatically. Early reinstatement requests must go through documented compliance review — not via this screen.

Audit log · regulator hand-off

Open the Audit log from the sidebar. Append-only, hash-chained log of every state change since boot.

  • Filter by category (AUTH, WALLET, BET, ADMIN, COMPLIANCE, SECURITY).
  • Verify chain — green tick = tamper-evident integrity end-to-end.
  • Export a signed CSV — same shape you hand the regulator.

System

System · health & controls

Open the System tile from the sidebar. Uptime, rate-limit buckets, SMS provider success, version. Backup & restore controls.

TOTP for staff. First sign-in prompts you to scan a QR; every later sign-in asks for a 6-digit authenticator code.

Live demo walk-through

Show a win in five minutes

The platform ships with a handful of Demo markets that close in 5, 15, 30 minutes and a few hours, so you can show the full bet → settle → celebration loop in one session.

1 · Open as the player

Sign in on a phone or a second browser. On the Markets screen, tap the Ending soon filter. Pick a market labelled Demo · 5-minute and place a YES bet.

You'll get a centered confirmation popup with a 5-second locked-in price, then a centered green checkmark "Bet placed" card. Your stake leaves the wallet immediately.

2 · Switch to the operator console

On the same laptop (or a separate admin browser), open the Resolver queue tile. The demo market now sits at the top — it's already inside the 24-hour window.

Press Resolve YES. A toast confirms Stage 1 recorded · awaiting second officer. The same officer cannot confirm twice — that's the two-officer rule, server-enforced.

3 · Settle as a second officer

Open a different admin browser (incognito or a colleague's account). Same Resolver queue, same market — press Resolve YES again. A claret confirm dialog appears: "This is final · payouts credit, positions close, no undo · Hatua hii haiwezi kubatilishwa." Press Yes, settle YES — settlement fires, payouts credit, positions close, an entry lands in the audit log.

4 · Watch the celebration on the player

Within a few seconds the player browser pops the celebration overlay — gold confetti, the payout figure, a "View positions" CTA. The notification bell shows You won. The wallet pill in the top bar updates live.

For the loss path, repeat with a NO bet on the same market — the player gets the kit "Pool grew" notification (no fanfare, by design).

Audit hand-off. Open the Audit log from the sidebar after settlement — the market.resolve.stage1, market.resolved, BET_PAYOUT entries are right there, hash-chained, with a one-click Verify chain button.

Sign out cleanly

Avatar menu (top-right) → Sign out. A confirmation dialog ensures you don't get bumped out by a misclick. Session is invalidated immediately and a session-destroyed entry is logged.

Lost your authenticator app? Contact the security lead with the AML lead in copy. A reset is logged as an admin action and requires written justification.
