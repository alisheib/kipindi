Mapigo · Signature Mini-Game · For Kipindi

The match has a pulse.
Now you can bet it.

Mapigo (Swahili: "heartbeats") is the signature in-platform mini-game for Kipindi. Players watch a live intensity waveform of a real football match and call the next 60 seconds: will the pulse SPIKE, DRIFT, or stay CALM? All stakes pool together; winners share by stake share — same fairness math as the rest of Kipindi.

"Feel the match. Bet the pulse." · "Hisi mechi. Cheza mapigo."

01 How a round plays
STEP 01
Watch the pulse
A gold waveform on a live match — spikes when something exciting happens, calm in midfield.
STEP 02
Call the next 60s
SPIKE (big peak coming), DRIFT (gradual rise/fall), or CALM (no big events).
STEP 03
Stake into the pool
All stakes pool together. One bet per round. Same fairness model as Kipindi core.
STEP 04
Winners share
Round resolves; winners split the pool by stake share. New round opens immediately.

Why we should ship this

  • No competitor has it. Aviator and JetX are RNG crash games. We tie play to a real live match feed — same data, second product surface.
  • Reuses platform infra. Built on the live match feed already in scope; the PoolPulseRing already lives in our design system.
  • Educational. Players learn to read momentum — that deepens their main-product time-window betting too.
  • Shareable visual. The hub-tile (gold waveform on starfield) is built to go viral on TZ Twitter / WhatsApp.

Designed to be safe

  • 60-second cycles. Short, addictive, bounded — no whales destroying themselves in one session.
  • One bet per round. Hard rule. The tray locks on placement.
  • Loss is dignified. No "you lost" — uses Kipindi's "the pool grew" framing per voice guide §2.17.
  • Inherits all RG controls. Daily limits, self-exclusion, reality checks, helpline footer — all present from day one.
