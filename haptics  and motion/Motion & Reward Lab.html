Haptics, micro-motion & achievements

A tactile, alive, rewarding layer — built entirely on the kit you already ship. No new colours, fonts, radii, shadows, or motion tokens. Every animation replays here; every haptic fires on tap, with a pulse visualizer so the pattern is legible without a phone.

Tokens only EN · SW bilingual 60fps · transform/opacity Reduced-motion + off-switch

How I approached it

I treated this as completing the kit, not extending it. Three systems, one family: haptics are punctuation on the moments that matter; micro-motion makes the everyday feel responsive; badges are a sibling to TierBadge — milestones, not rank. The win-reveal is weighted heaviest: it must feel earned, never slot-machine.

Do-not-do — confirmed

  • No new colour palettes, fonts, radii, or shadow scales. All values are existing OKLCH tokens.
  • No parallel motion system — micro-motion reuses kit keyframes (reveal-up, count-up-flash, seal-impress…). Zero new motion tokens.
  • No full-colour illustration, mascots, or emoji. Badges are heraldic line-art, gilt-on-royal.
  • No compulsion loops. We celebrate outcomes & skill, never nudge "play again".
  • Every keyframe ships a prefers-reduced-motion branch; haptics + motion are user-disablable.

Request 01

Haptics system

Seven named tokens mapped to product events. On Android/Chrome they fire the Web Vibration API; on iOS Safari they no-op and the paired animation carries the moment. Tap any token to feel it (on a supported device) and watch the pulse visualizer beat the exact vibrate() pattern.

iOS fallback

  • navigator.vibrate is absent in Safari — fire() silently no-ops.
  • Design so the animation always carries the moment (win-burst, seal-impress, value-flash) — feedback never depends on the motor.
  • Detected once via feature-check; no try/catch on the hot path.

Restraint rules

  • Haptics are punctuation, not a drumbeat. No buzz on every tap or scroll.
  • Default ON for money / outcome moments; OFF for routine taps & selects.
  • Loss gets no haptic — dignified, never punitive.
  • celebrate is reserved for the genuine peak.
